Class: Backpack

Attributes:
color (string)
total_capacity (integer)
current_capacity (integer)
zipped (boolean)
pins (array)


Methods:
fill_backpack (total_capacity - current_capacity and fill the remaining amount)
unzip (if zipped (attribute) equals true, unzip the backpack)
dye_backpack (if color (attribute) equals "white" then dye backpack "blue")
add_pins (add a backpack pin to the Pins array)
